Production Guidelines
How Production Works
  1. Select a recipe
  2. Enter quantity to produce
  3. Set unit cost (or leave blank for auto-calculation)
  4. Assign a lot number
  5. System will:
    • Check ingredient availability
    • Deduct ingredients from stock
    • Add finished goods to inventory
Unit Cost

If you leave the unit cost blank, the system will automatically calculate it based on the recipe's ingredient costs. You can also manually enter a cost if you want to include labor, overhead, or other expenses.

Important
  • Ensure sufficient ingredients are in stock
  • Lot numbers must be unique
  • Production cannot be undone
  • All ingredient consumption will be recorded
